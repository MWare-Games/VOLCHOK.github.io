About the Game

VOLCHOK is a psychological horror game set in 1994 within an abandoned Soviet communications facility buried deep in Siberia. Players must uncover long-forgotten Cold War secrets while evading a colossal creature born of failed experiments and paranoia.

Features

  • Atmospheric Soviet horror setting
  • Minimalist, eerie piano score
  • Immersive exploration and survival mechanics
